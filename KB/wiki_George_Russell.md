Biography
[
]
Not much is known yet about George's origins or family background, but the impression is that he's largely a self-made man. He's become a multimillionaire with the close assistance of his dear wife
Bertha Russell
. At some point, they had lost a fortune, but managed to make it again. He is the head of business at his company Russell Consolidated Trust. His wealth comes largely from his ownership of railway business.
He moved to New York City with his family around 1879, and they moved into their
palatial estate
on the 61st Street in summer 1882, when season 1 took place.
He has a close and loving relationship with Bertha, and is also a kind father to his son
Larry Russell
, and especially to his daughter
Gladys Russell
.
