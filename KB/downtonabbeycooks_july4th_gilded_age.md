# A Tantalizing Tour of July 4th Foods From the Gilded Age
Source: https://www.downtonabbeycooks.com/a-tantalizing-tour-of-favourite-july-4th-foods-in-the-gilded-age/
Author: Pamela Foster (Downton Abbey Cooks)

## Gilded Age July 4th Celebrations

Streets featured "elaborate floats adorned with patriotic symbols, such as American flags, eagles, and Lady Liberty." Buildings displayed red, white, and blue bunting. Spectacular pyrotechnic displays "reflected the opulence and excesses of the Gilded Age."

Families gathered in parks and countryside locations for picnics featuring abundant, indulgent foods.

## Featured July 4th Foods of the Gilded Age

### Savory Dishes
- Fried chicken — "the centrepiece of the picnic spread"
- Potato salad
- Deviled eggs
- Coleslaw
- Saratoga chips (one of the first potato chips)

### Sweet Treats
- Apple, cherry, and blueberry pies with fresh seasonal fruits
- Ice cream in various flavors served with cones or wafers

Food played an essential role in creating a sense of community and indulgence during Independence Day celebrations of the era.
